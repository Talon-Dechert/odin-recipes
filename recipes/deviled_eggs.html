Deviled Eggs

Description

Hard boiled eggs stuffed with creamy blend of mayonnaise, Dijon mustard and rice wine vinegar. Fresh dill and garlic powder add a delightful flavor.

Ingredients

  • 6 hard boiled eggs, halved
  • 1 teaspoon rice wine vinegar
  • 1/2 teaspoon chopped fresh dill
  • 1 teaspoon Dijon mustard
  • 1/4 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/8 teaspoon salt
  • 12 sprigs fresh dill

Steps

  1. Scoop egg yolks into a bowl and set egg whites aside.
  2. Mash yolks, mayonnaise, vinegar, 1/2 teaspoon chopped dill, Dijon mustard, garlic powder, and salt.
  3. Spoon yolk mixture into egg whites. Garnish with dill sprigs. Refrigerate until ready to serve, cover for best preservation.
