Baked Spaghetti

Description

A delicious and easy comfort dinner that the whole family will love! Made with spaghetti sauce, mozzarella and cream cheese.

Ingredients

  • 1 (16 ounce) package uncooked spaghetti
  • 2 (24 ounce) cans of Traditional Spaghetti sauce
  • 2 cups mozzarella cheese
  • 1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C)
  2. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Cook spaghetti in the boiliing water, stirring occasionally until cooked through but firm to the bite, about 12 minutes. Drain.
  3. Heat the sauce in the saucepan over medium heat. Add the cream cheese and stir until melted. Pour pasta and sauce into an oven-safe baking dish and mix well.
  4. Bake for 30 minutes. Remove from oven and top with mozzarella cheese. Bake until the sauce is thick and the cheese in melted, 10 to 15 minutes.
